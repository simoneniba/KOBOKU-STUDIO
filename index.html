The Philosophy.

Koboku — 枯木 — is one of the most celebrated ink paintings by Miyamoto Musashi, the undefeated swordsman who sought mastery through art as much as combat. A withered tree rendered with absolute precision. Economy of form. Intentionality in every stroke.

That is how we approach AI creative direction: disciplined vision, no wasted motion, results that land. We are not prompt engineers. We are strategic multipliers — directors who understand that the brief is only the beginning.

The Founders

Simone Niba

Co-Founder — Creative Director

Born and raised in Florence. Graduate in Business Administration from the University of Florence. World-class kendo athlete and current member of the Italian National Team, training to compete in the 2027 Kendo World Championship in Japan.

Simone has developed his creative eye through music — under the project Lord Falco — and through hands-on visual work in Los Angeles alongside Sam Homaee, and in Florence with videomaker Giovanni De Chiara. He brings strategic vision, cultural range, and the discipline of an elite competitor to every project.

Bruno Di Felice

Co-Founder — Technology & Strategy

Also a kendo practitioner and Florence native. Bruno brings a sharp advertising background and deep technical fluency to the studio — the architecture behind the creative. He architects the systems, tools, and workflows that make Koboku's output scalable.

Simone and Bruno have been friends and training partners since kindergarten. That shared foundation — discipline, trust, and a competitive drive to win — is at the core of how Koboku operates.
