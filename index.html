The Philosophy.

Koboku — 枯木 — is one of the most celebrated ink paintings by Miyamoto Musashi, the undefeated swordsman who sought mastery through art as much as combat. A withered tree rendered with absolute precision. Economy of form. Intentionality in every stroke.

That is how we approach AI creative direction: disciplined vision, no wasted motion, results that land. We are not prompt engineers. We are strategic multipliers — directors who understand that the brief is only the beginning.
